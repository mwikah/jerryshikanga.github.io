Innovest

Where innovators and investors meet

New

Many innovators fail to get their products to prodcution due to insufficent resources to start. At the same time many investors would like to invest but they are locked out but the current platforms such as the stock exchange markets due to limits on the minimum one can invest. This therefore creates a virtual barrier thus killing many bright ideas.

Innovest comes in to break this barrier. It takes the worry of how to raise funds from the innovator and let him focus on what matters most : developing the product.
